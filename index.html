Mission

Connection through thoughtful communication to deepen relationships, tell compelling stories, bring the community together, and‚Äîabove all‚Äîhave fun.

Backyard Players, Inc. is a group of unique, caring, and creative individuals who come together to imagine, develop, and produce live outdoor theatrical productions for family, friends, and community members. We believe that theater can be joyful, local, and accessible. By producing professional-quality performances in an intimate backyard setting, we make the experience of live theater personal and affordable while strengthening neighborhood bonds through creativity and laughter.

Background

Founded in 2022 by six friends who met performing musical theater in Oakland.
